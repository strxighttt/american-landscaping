Built by Diaz

Diaz Landscape & Construction LLC was founded on a simple belief: hardscaping should be as functional as it is beautiful. For nearly two decades, we've served Maryland homeowners and contractors with precision stonework that stands the test of time.

Every wall, patio, and walkway we build is backed by meticulous planning, quality materials, and a crew that takes pride in the craft. We don't cut corners — we set stone.

Our Process

From concept to completion, we keep every phase organized and transparent.

1

Site Assessment

We visit your property to take measurements, evaluate soil and drainage, and discuss your vision.

2

Proposal

You receive a detailed quote with material options, timeline, and a 3D concept rendering.

3

Construction

Our crew executes the build with precision — from base prep and grading to final installation.

4

Final Walk

We review the completed work together, answer questions, and provide care and maintenance tips.
