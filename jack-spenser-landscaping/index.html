About Jack Spenser

Jack Spenser has been a trusted name in Silver Spring landscaping for over 25 years. Founded as a small family operation, we have grown through word of mouth and repeat business by doing one thing well — creating beautiful, lasting landscapes. We believe in showing up on time, using the best materials, and treating every yard as if it were our own. No shortcuts, no gimmicks. Just solid landscaping from people who care.
