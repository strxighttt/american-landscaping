About Timothy's Garden

A tradition of tender care since 1998

Timothy's Garden Inc has been nurturing Silver Spring gardens for over two decades. We believe a garden is a living artwork — one that responds to gentle attention and informed care.

Our approach is unhurried and meticulous. We select every plant for its place, prune with an artist's eye, and tend each garden as if it were our own. From intimate cottage gardens to sweeping estate grounds, we bring the same dedication to every project.

Let us help your garden flourish.
