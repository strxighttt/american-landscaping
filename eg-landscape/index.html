Rooted in Nature

E&G Landscape was founded on the belief that great landscaping works with nature, not against it. We specialize in sustainable, ecologically minded designs that reduce water use, support biodiversity, and create lasting beauty.

Every project begins with observation — understanding the light, soil, water flow, and existing ecology of your property. Then we design with intention, selecting plants and materials that will thrive naturally with minimal intervention.

— E&G Landscape, designing with nature since 2010

Our Approach

A thoughtful process rooted in ecological understanding and collaborative design.

1

Observe

We study your property's microclimate, soil, drainage, and existing plant communities to inform every decision.

2

Plan

We create a detailed design concept with plant palettes, material selections, and a phased implementation timeline.

3

Install

Our crew brings the design to life with careful installation, minimal waste, and respect for the existing landscape.

4

Grow

We provide ongoing support and maintenance guidance to help your landscape mature and thrive naturally.
