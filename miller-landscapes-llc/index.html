About Mark Willcher

Mark Willcher Landscape Design & Build is a Silver Spring-based studio specializing in residential landscapes that blend natural beauty with thoughtful design. From intimate garden retreats to full property transformations, every project is guided by a commitment to craft, ecology, and client collaboration. We don't just install plants and stone — we create outdoor spaces that feel like they belong.
