About Miller Landscapes LLC

Miller Landscapes LLC is a modern landscape design-build firm based in Silver Spring, MD. We focus on clean lines, sustainable materials, and functional outdoor spaces tailored to contemporary living. Our team brings together design expertise and construction precision on every project — from intimate courtyard gardens to full property transformations. We don't cut corners. We build landscapes that last.
