About Us

Rooted in Silver Spring since 2006

Rock Creek Landscaping Inc brings an organic, nature-first approach to every project. We believe the best landscapes are the ones that feel like they've always been there — seamlessly integrated with the surrounding environment.

Our team specializes in native plantings, natural stonework, and ecological design. We serve residential and commercial clients throughout Montgomery County, with a focus on sustainable practices and timeless aesthetics.

Let us help you create a landscape that belongs.
