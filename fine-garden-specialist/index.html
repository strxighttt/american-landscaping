About The Fine Garden Specialist

✷

With over a decade of experience in fine horticulture and garden design, we specialize in creating curated outdoor spaces for discerning homeowners in the Silver Spring area. Every project is approached with an artist’s eye and a gardener’s care, blending structure with softness, formality with charm. We believe a garden should feel both intentional and alive — a place of beauty throughout the seasons.
