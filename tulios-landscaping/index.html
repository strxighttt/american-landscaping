Where Tradition
Meets the Garden

Tulio's Landscaping brings generations of horticultural tradition to the Washington DC area. Our founder, Tulio Marchetti, learned the craft in the olive groves of southern Italy before bringing his passion to Silver Spring.

We believe every garden tells a story. Our designs weave together Mediterranean warmth with Mid-Atlantic ecology, creating spaces that feel both timeless and alive.

— Tulio Marchetti
