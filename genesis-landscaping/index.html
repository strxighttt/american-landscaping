About Genesis Landscaping

Building better landscapes since 2012

Genesis Landscaping was founded with the belief that great landscapes start with great design. Based in Silver Spring, we serve homeowners who value clean lines, sustainable practices, and modern aesthetics. Our team designs and builds every project with precision, using high-quality materials and proven techniques to create outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and functional for years to come.
