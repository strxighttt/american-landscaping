About Green Lake Landscaping

We are a family-owned landscaping company serving Silver Spring and surrounding communities. Our team brings years of hands-on experience in landscape design, hardscaping, and horticulture. We take pride in creating outdoor environments that are as functional as they are beautiful — from cozy backyard retreats to expansive front-yard transformations. Every project is built on trust, quality, and a genuine love for the craft.
