About Stone Garden

Craftsmen of natural stone

Stone Garden Landscaping is built on a passion for natural stone and timeless design. Every wall, patio, and path we craft is a reflection of our commitment to quality and permanence.

Based in Silver Spring, MD, we work with locally sourced stone to create landscapes that feel ancient and enduring. From intimate garden paths to sweeping terraces, our work is defined by clean lines and honest materials.

We build for the long haul — landscapes that only get better with age.
